About Me

Adding to my 7 years of experience in multimedia production, I also have professional experience in digital communications. My experience includes web development using HTML and CSS, corporate management of social media and word processing.


I’ve always had a passion for media production (Film & Events) but a few years ago I was invited to a filming of 8 out of 10 cats at Pinewood Studios, met and talked to friends in the TV industry and been a part of a couple TV projects which has given me an insight in TV production that has motivated me to look beyond some of the practical and logistical issues I had initially.


I will always enjoy studio productions and will feel comfortable in that environment because I volunteer as Director/Vision Mixer in a Tech/Media team that broadcasts a live feed in-house and online every Sunday. However I’ve seen bigger TV productions on main channels championing people with an extraordinary story and I think that’s great for many people from different communities, a couple of which I’m a part of, which is why I’d like to develop and get experience in producing unique, engaging content on TV and online.
